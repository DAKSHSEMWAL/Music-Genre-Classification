Music Genre Classifier

  • Genre Label Classes:

    Classical Hiphop Jazz Metal Pop Rock


  • Supports Audio files in all formats.


  • Uploads only 1MB of the file.


  • Toggle Single to output a single label. and Multi to output multiple labels. Multiple labels shows that the given input has features of multiple genres.
